Libracantus


Libracantus is a fantasy world where many of my games are set.
Aster is a towerdwelling wizard living in solitude trying to create the Philosopher's Stone.
in Libracantus is also a species of humanoids called Aetherians who are beings created by magic.
they are created by fusing 2 souls into a gem and granting it sentience meaning they can be created for many purposes,
despite what they are made for, they have free will to pursue whatever they wish to.
as seen in Aether Arena, many Aetherians study magic and combat styles.
